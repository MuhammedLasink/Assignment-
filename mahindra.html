MAHINDRA

Mahindra & Mahindra Limited is a leading Indian automobile company and part of the Mahindra Group. Established in 1945, the company is well known for producing strong and reliable vehicles, especially SUVs and utility vehicles. Mahindra's popular car models include the Mahindra Thar, Scorpio, XUV700, and Bolero. It is also a pioneer in electric vehicles in India through its EV arm, Mahindra Electric. Known for rugged design and off-road capabilities, Mahindra cars are widely used in both urban and rural areas.
