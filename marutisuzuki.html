MARUTI SUZUKI

Maruti Suzuki India Limited is the largest car manufacturer in India. It was established in 1981 as Maruti Udyog Limited and later became a joint venture with the Japanese company Suzuki Motor Corporation. Maruti Suzuki is known for making affordable, fuel-efficient, and reliable cars. Popular models include the Alto, Swift, WagonR, Baleno, and Brezza. The company has a wide service network and a strong presence across the country. Maruti Suzuki has played a major role in making cars accessible to the average Indian family.
